ABOUT ME:
I have been creating art and digital content for live production sets and video games since 2012. Growing up in a family of artists and more musicaly minded siblings, I often equated the music they shared with me to the creation of new ideas. School and international travel in the later years of and after college changed my mind completely of what ideas could be derived from. With so much historic architecture and landscapes while at the same time, differences in people groups around the world in not only wealth, and history, but the way they derive joy from everyday activities and family time. Since then I have always jumped at the chance to observe or take part in different cultural experiences as well as be able to travel as much as possible.

I hope to move some place colder than Florida one day.
